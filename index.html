About Us

At KindKart, we aim to create a bridge between generosity and necessity. Our mission is to minimize food wastage and combat hunger by connecting food donors with nearby NGOs and volunteers through smart, location-based solutions.

Whether you’re a restaurant, household, or event host with surplus food, or a nonprofit organization or volunteer looking to serve your community— KindKart provides an intuitive and efficient way to give back.

Together, we can make a difference—one meal, one act of kindness at a time.
